**Future exits** | Individual LLCs can be sold (e.g., VPC to gaming company) without selling BX3 core or Agentic

## V. TAX STRATEGY: R&D FOCUS

### Government Funding Tax Treatment

| Grant Type | Tax Status | Strategy |
|------------|------------|----------|
**SBIR Phase I/II** | Taxable income (federal) | Offset with R&D expenses; carryforward losses  
**STTR** | Taxable income | Same; university partner gets separate tax treatment  
**ARPA-E** | Taxable income | High R&D burn rate → minimal net taxable income  
**USDA NIFA** | Taxable income | Ag-specific equipment depreciation accelerated  

### Key Tax Positions

1. **R&D Tax Credit (Section 41)**
   - Agentic development = qualified research (technological uncertainty, experimentation, software)
   - 20% of qualified expenses as credit
   - $250K+ annual credit potential for 2-engineer team

2. **Section 179/179D (Energy Efficiency)**
   - Irrig8 sensor network = energy monitoring equipment
   - Immediate deduction for hardware deployment

3. **QBI Deduction (199A)**
   - Build-A-Biz LLC income may qualify (service business limitation applies)
   - Strategy: Keep W-2 wages reasonable to maximize QBI

4. **Delaware Franchise Tax**
   - Authorized shares method (minimize par value)
   ~$400/year annual cost

### State Tax Considerations

### Employment Agreement Requirements

All employees and contractors must sign:

1. **IP Assignment Agreement**
   - All work product owned by BX3 Inc
   - No side projects using company resources
   - Post-employment IP transfer obligations

2. **Confidentiality Agreement (NDA)**
   - Agentic specifically named as trade secret
   - No reverse engineering
   - 5-year post-employment confidentiality

3. **Non-Compete (where enforceable: CO, DE, NV)**
   - 12-month non-compete in AI orchestration
   - Enforceable in Delaware; limited in Colorado

4. **Exit Protocol**
   - Immediate access revocation
   - Return of all materials
   - Exit interview + re-reminder of obligations
