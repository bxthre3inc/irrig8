## Deterministic AI Workforce for Small Business

## Company
Build-A-Biz AI Operations (Bxthre3 Inc)

## Tagline
AI agents that run your business. You run your craft.

## Problem
Small business owners in skilled trades (painters, cleaners, landscapers, detailers, handymen) spend 60% of their time on admin — not billable work. A $75/hr painter loses $1,875/week to $0/hr admin. Annual cost: $93,750 in foregone revenue.

They can't afford assistants. Can't figure out software. Can't keep up. So they cap their income at their admin capacity, not their skill.

## Solution
Build-A-Biz installs 3 AI agents:

1. **Inbox Agent** — triages inquiries, drafts responses, books appointments, flags urgents
2. **Marketing Agent** — posts to social, responds to reviews, runs local SEO, manages ads
3. **Ops Agent** — invoices, payment follow-up, supply orders, time tracking, weekly summaries

The owner gets a phone. Morning: 3 tasks. Do, mark done, paint. Sunday: 15-min briefing. That's it.
