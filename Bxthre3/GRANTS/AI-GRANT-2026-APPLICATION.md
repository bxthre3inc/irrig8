## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Investor Protector is a self-proving dashboard that tracks the velocity, productivity, and capital efficiency of Bxthre3 Inc's AI-native venture studio. Unlike traditional startup reporting (quarterly updates, subjective narratives), Investor Protector provides real-time, deterministic metrics that cannot be gamed or fudged.

**The Problem:** Early-stage investors have no visibility into whether a startup is actually executing or merely presenting. Founders optimize for pitch decks, not operational truth.

**Our Solution:** A transparency layer that pulls directly from code commits, task completion, document creation, and cost tracking — no human filtering, no quarterly spin.
