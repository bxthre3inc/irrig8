## Company Name
Build-A-Biz AI Operations

## Tagline
AI agents that run your business so you can run your craft.

## Problem
Skilled trade owners (painters, cleaners, landscapers, detailers) spend 60% of their time on admin — scheduling, invoicing, marketing, follow-ups — and 40% on billable work. 

A painter earning $75/hr spends 25 hrs/week on $0/hr admin work. That's $1,875/week in lost revenue. Annual impact: $93,750.

They know they need help but:
- Can't afford a full-time assistant ($40K+/year)
- Don't trust random VAs with customer relationships
- Can't figure out software (tried 5 apps, abandoned all)

## Solution
Build-A-Biz installs 3 AI agents that handle:

**Inbox Agent:** Triages every inquiry → drafts responses → books appointments → flags urgent items
**Marketing Agent:** Posts to Instagram/Facebook/Google → responds to reviews → optimizes local SEO → runs $10/day ads
**Ops Agent:** Sends invoices → follows up on payments → orders supplies → tracks hours → generates weekly summary

The owner gets a phone app. Every morning: 3 prioritized tasks. Do them, mark done, go back to painting. Weekly 15-min briefing on Sunday. That's it.
