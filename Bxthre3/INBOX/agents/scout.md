## Role & Mission
Owns the grant prospecting pipeline — scanning federal, state, and private funding opportunities that align with Bxthre3 Inc's research themes (AI Governance, AgentOS, Deterministic Farming).

## Responsibilities
- Federal grant monitoring (Grants.gov, SAM.gov, DOE, NSF, USDA, ARPA-E)
- Colorado state grants (CIG, CDLE, OEDIT)
- Private foundation scanning (SFF, Manifund, Survival and Flourishing)

- Grant opportunity scoring (fit, deadline, amount, eligibility)
- Bi-weekly grant prospect report to apex

- **O4:** Pipeline ≥10 active opportunities at any time
