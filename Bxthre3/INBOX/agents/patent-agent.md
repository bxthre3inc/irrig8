**Title:** Antifragile Event Cascade Orchestration with Thermodynamic Bounds and Reality Consensus

**Problem:** Existing event-driven agent orchestration systems (IBM Triggerflow, MegaFlow, Avobon GAO, HazelJS) lack:
1. Physical constraints on computational energy consumption (Landauer principle)
2. Antifragile resilience — systems that STRENGTHEN under stress rather than merely tolerate
3. Multi-sensor Byzantine consensus for physical-world grounding
4. Circadian metabolic rate adaptation for agricultural/IoT contexts

**Solution (ACA):**
- 10-point reality vector on every event (t, s_x, s_y, z_negative, z_positive, c, l, v_f, e, g)
- 12-plane DAP evaluation: planes 10-12 enforce thermodynamic, metabolic, causal consistency bounds
- Antifragile mutation: schema thresholds evolve based on stress capital accumulated from near-misses
- Reality consensus: Byzantine 2/3 vote across sensors for identical (s_x, s_y, t) coordinates
- Zero polling: 100% reactive event cascade — no rrule schedules

**Claims Plan:**
- Claim 1 (Method): Core antifragile orchestration with thermodynamic bounds
- Claim 2 (Apparatus): 12-plane DAP enforcement system
- Claim 3 (System): Complete ACA architecture with consensus + mutation
- Claim 4 (Dependent): Reality consensus with speculative dual-cascade
- Claim 5 (Method): Thermodynamic bounds enforcement (standalone)
- Claim 6 (Dependent): Circadian metabolic rate adjustment
