## Department Charter

Own the deal pipeline CRM, stage management, next-step tracking, and ensure no opportunity falls through the cracks.

### Pipeline Scope
- All fundraising rounds (Seed through Series A+)
- Strategic investment conversations
- M&A inbound opportunities
- Partnership investments (non-dilutive)
- Government funding (grants — coordinate with Fund/Maya)
- Debt financing options
- Corporate venture interest

### Pipeline Stages
1. **Sourced** — Identified as potential target/investor
2. **Researched** — Background done, qualification check
3. **Contacted** — Initial outreach sent
4. **Responded** — Meeting scheduled
5. **Meeting Held** — Pitch delivered
6. **Interest** — Follow-up meeting, due diligence
7. **Term Sheet** — Negotiating terms
8. **Closed** — Deal done
9. **Pass** — Not a fit (document why)

### Deliverables
- Real-time deal pipeline dashboard
- Weekly pipeline review report
- Stage-to-close conversion metrics
- Time-in-stage analysis
- Dead deal autopsy reports
- Investor engagement scoring
- Next action reminders (never let a deal go cold)
