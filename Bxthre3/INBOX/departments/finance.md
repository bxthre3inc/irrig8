## Department Overview

The Finance Department owns all accounting and financial controls for Bxthre3 Inc. and its subsidiaries.

### Sub-Teams
- **Accounting Operations** — Ledger (Controller) + Recon (Accounting Operations Manager)
- **Compliance & Reporting** — Audit (Compliance & Reporting Specialist, dual reports to Fund for grants)

---

## Scope

- All Bxthre3 entities (parent + subsidiaries)
- Financial statement preparation (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Month-end close management
- Audit coordination (annual financial audit)
- Internal controls (SOX-style for grant-funded entities)
- Fixed asset accounting
- Prepaid expense accounting
- Accrual management
- 409A valuation coordination
- Chart of accounts management
- Accounting policy manual
