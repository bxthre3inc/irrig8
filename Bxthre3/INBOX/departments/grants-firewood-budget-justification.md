## Why This Budget Wins

1. **~50% goes directly to SLV households** (subsidies + supplier jobs + incentives)
2. **~40% builds permanent capacity** (platform, training, legal structure)
3. **~10% administrative** (well within USDA norms)
4. **Match is real** — already-built software, founder commitment
