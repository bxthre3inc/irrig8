## Purpose

Standardized framework for evaluating, scoring, and prioritizing partnership opportunities across all Bxthre3 ventures.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

### Tier 1 — Strategic Fit (Must Have)

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Mission alignment** | 25% | 1–5 (1=competitor, 3=neutral, 5=adjacent/complementary) |
| **Market access** | 20% | 1–5 (distribution reach, customer access, geographic overlap) |
| **Data access** | 15% | 1–5 (proprietary data, API access, integration depth) |

### Tier 2 — Business Value

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Revenue potential** | 15% | 1–5 (direct revenue, referral revenue, cost reduction) |
| **Competitive positioning** | 10% | 1–5 (barriers to entry, lock-in, moat enhancement) |
| **Brand amplification** | 10% | 1–5 (credibility, reach, co-marketing potential) |

### Tier 3 — Operational

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Technical integration complexity** | 5% | 1–5 (1=easy API, 5=custom/hard) |
| **Regulatory exposure** | 0% | Binary (0=no new risk, 1=new regulatory surface) |

**Total Score: 100 points max**

---

## Partnership Categories

### Category A — Distribution Partners
- **Definition:** Partners that sell/resell to end customers
- **Examples:** Dealers, retailers, resellers, agents
- **Evaluation Focus:** Market access, revenue share, territorial protection

### Category B — Technology Partners
- **Definition:** Partners providing API, integrations, co-development
- **Examples:** Data providers, platform integrations, co-dev studios
- **Evaluation Focus:** Data access, technical complexity, strategic exclusivity

### Category C — Channel Partners
- **Definition:** Partners that drive customer acquisition
- **Examples:** Affiliates, SaaS distributors, platform aggregators
- **Evaluation Focus:** Volume/cost per acquisition, brand fit, compliance

### Category D — Strategic Investors
- **Definition:** Equity investors with strategic value
- **Examples:** AgTech VCs, gaming investors, corporate VCs
- **Evaluation Focus:** Term valuation, strategic resources, board seat

## Stage Gates

| Stage | Gate Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| **1. Prospecting** | Target identified, initial research complete | Contact info obtained |
| **2. Outreach** | Initial email/call sent, response received | Meeting scheduled |
| **3. Qualification** | 1+ meeting complete, fit confirmed | NDA signed |
| **4. Negotiation** | Term sheet drafted | Term sheet agreed |
| **5. Signed** | Contract executed | Partnership active |
| **6. Active** | Partnership live | Quarterly review |

---

## Decision Rules

- **P1:** Score ≥70 AND Stage ≥2 → Immediate priority
- **P2:** Score 50–69 AND Stage ≥2 → Short-term pipeline
- **P3:** Score <50 OR Stage =1 → Monitor / passive outreach
- **Pass:** Any partner scoring <40 on mission alignment → Decline
