## NRCS Monte Vista Field Office — EQIP Vendor Enrollment

**Source:** NRCS Service Center Locator — nrcs.usda.gov/contact/find-a-service-center

**Process for EQIP vendor enrollment:**
1. Contact **local NRCS field office** (Monte Vista) to express interest as a technology vendor
2. NRCS Monte Vista: **(719) 589-4841** (from INBOX records)
3. Ask about becoming an approved **Technical Service Provider (TSP)** for irrigation technology
4. TSP approval allows Irrig8 to be **recommended by NRCS** to farmers as part of their EQIP contract work
5. This is **recurring revenue** — every farmer who gets an EQIP contract for irrigation tech can use Irrig8

**Key point:** EQIP vendor enrollment is different from EQIP funding. Bxthre3 would be a **vendor/TSP**, not an EQIP applicant. This means:
- No competitive application process
- Farmers choose Irrig8 as their contractor under their own EQIP contract
- Revenue is recurring and direct
