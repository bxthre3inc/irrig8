## The C + B Insight

**You don't need to choose between protecting all your IP and writing strong papers.**

arXiv and Zenodo serve different roles — and combining them creates a stronger trajectory than either alone.

**Key reframe:** Research Papers (40-55 pages, high impact, heavily cited) + Technical Specifications (12-25 pages, IP protection, still citable) = optimal combination.

## Zenodo vs arXiv Strategy

**Zenodo first (all papers):**
- Immediate publication, no gatekeeping
- DOIs assigned instantly
- Timestamp evidence of prior art
- 4-6 week arXiv endorsement window

**Then arXiv (4 strong papers):**
- BX3 Framework, Bailout Protocol, Forensic Ledger, Recursive Spawning
- After 4-6 weeks, with improved citations
- Builds on Zenodo downloads/citations

**After 6 months:**
- Real citations and downloads demonstrated
- Credibility established
- arXiv endorsement becomes easy
- Complete publication portfolio ready
