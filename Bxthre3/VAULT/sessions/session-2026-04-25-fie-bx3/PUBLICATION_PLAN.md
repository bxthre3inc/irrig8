## The C+B Insight

> **You don't need to choose between protecting all your IP and writing strong papers.**

arXiv + Zenodo = optimal combination. Zenodo for first-priority timestamps and DOI assignment (no endorsement barrier), arXiv for high-visibility research papers and conference submissions. 8 simultaneous publications without diminishing impact when structured correctly.

**Better path than arXiv-only:**
- Permanent DOIs immediately
- Better versioning
- Open peer review path
- Stronger credibility arc: **Zenodo → arXiv → conferences**
- No endorsement dependency at submission stage
