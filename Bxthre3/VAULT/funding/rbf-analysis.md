|--------|-----|--------|
| **Non-dilutive** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No — dilution occurs |

| **Best for** | Growth with existing revenue | Pre-launch, scaling, acquisitions |

| Monthly Revenue | Revenue Share | Monthly Payment | Months to Repay | Status |
