## NOTES FOR INDEPENDENT RESEARCHER OUTREACH

- **Email best practices:** Reference specific papers; describe how your research agenda intersects with theirs; mention Irrig8/AgentOS resource health management context
- **Sponsorship language:** Frame as seeking "collaboration" or "mentorship" rather than formal sponsorship — many faculty are more receptive to research partnerships than formal sponsorship
- **Multi-agent framing:** If reaching out on governance, emphasize multi-agent coordination, auditability, and deterministic operating systems (Irrig8's core value prop)
- **Colorado first:** Prioritize Colorado-based faculty before expanding nationally — in-person meetings and regional collaboration are lower friction
- **Jacob Haimes + Patrick Martinez Peel:** Treat as peer collaborators, not mentors — they are independent researchers who may be as interested in BX3's work as BX3 is in theirs
