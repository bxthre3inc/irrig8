## Overview

Valley Players Club affiliate program is the primary acquisition channel. This doc defines the affiliate structure, commission tiers, compliance requirements, and operational workflow.

---

## Affiliate Types

### Tier 1: Affiliate Managers
**Profile:** Individual or company managing multiple affiliate sites/players
**Commission:** 30% revenue share
**Typical Volume:** Medium (10-50 players/month)
**Compliance:** Standard gaming affiliate disclosure, state-specific licensing

### Tier 2: Casino Aggregators
**Profile:** Platforms aggregating multiple sweepstakes/casino offerings
**Commission:** 25% revenue share
**Typical Volume:** High (50-500 players/month)
**Compliance:** Master affiliate agreement, sub-affiliate terms

### Tier 3: Individual Promoters
**Profile:** Social media influencers, content creators
**Commission:** CPA model ($50-100 per active player)
**Typical Volume:** Low-medium
**Compliance:** FTC disclosure, platform terms

## Affiliate Lifecycle

```
1. RECRUITMENT
   → Outbound: affiliate managers, aggregators
   → Inbound: affiliate application portal

2. ONBOARDING
   → Application review (24-48 hrs)
   → Agreement signing (digital)
   → Account setup + tracking link generation
   → Orientation + creative assets

3. ACTIVATION
   → Provide marketing assets (banners, links, copy)
   → Assign affiliate manager (for Tier 1/2)
   → Set performance baseline

4. OPTIMIZATION
   → Monthly performance review
   → Commission tier adjustments (quarterly)
   → Top performer recognition

5. PAYMENT
   → Monthly reconciliation
   → Invoice processing
   → Payment disbursement
```
