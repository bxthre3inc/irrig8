# Strategy: Stakeholder Identification & Mapping

To secure the \"First 5 Fields\" for the June 29th Trial, we must map
the intersection of land ownership, water debt, and legal
representation.

## 1. Identifying the Attorneys (The Gatekeepers)

The \"Notice of Evidence Availability\" should be sent to the lead
counsels managing the Subdistrict 1 Adaptive Management Plan (AMP)
litigation.

-   **Public Record Search:** Access the **Colorado Water Court
    > (Division 3)** filings. Look specifically for the \"Case
    > Management Order\" related to the 2026 Subdistrict 1 Trial.

-   **Key Firms to Look For:** \* *The Subdistrict Counsel:* Often David
    > Robbins (Hill & Robbins) or similar specialized water firms in
    > Denver/Alamosa.

    -   *Objector Counsel:* Identify the attorneys representing the
        > \"Sustainable Water Users\" or individual large-scale ditch
        > companies who are challenging the current management plan.

-   **The Pitch:** We provide the attorneys with the **QCV Report**,
    > which is the only evidence that moves their arguments from
    > \"expert opinion\" to \"deterministic fact.\"

## 2. Identifying the Farmers (The Early Adopters)

We need farmers with high \"Water Debt\" (those paying the highest
per-acre-foot over-pumping fees).

-   **The \"Quota List\":** Use the **Rio Grande Water Conservation
    > District (RGWCD)** public database. Look for operations with:

    -   High pumping volumes (\>1,000 acre-feet/year).

    -   Recent history of \"Over-pumping\" fees.

    -   Proximity to the \"Confined Aquifer\" sensitive zones.

-   **The \"Mud\" Intelligence:** Local interaction at the **Monte Vista
    > Co-op** or the **Potato Administrative Committee**. In the valley,
    > 80% of the water is controlled by 20% of the families.

## 3. The \"Discovery\" Ledger (Internal Data Collection)

Create a CRM for the **Foundry Protocol** to track:

1.  **APN (Assessor Parcel Number):** Cross-referenced with our **SDW
    > (Librarian)** satellite data.

2.  **Current Legal Status:** Is this farmer currently an \"Objector\"
    > or a \"Supporter\" of the plan?

3.  **Hardware Readiness:** Does the farm have a center-pivot with an
    > existing Modbus/GPIO interface for the Hub?

## 4. Immediate Outreach Action

-   **Step 1:** Request the \"Service List\" for Case No. \[Current
    > Water Case Number\] from the Division 3 Water Clerk. This contains
    > the email/address of every attorney involved.

-   **Step 2:** Direct mail the **Notice of Evidence Availability**
    > specifically to the *Technical Experts* hired by these firms.
    > Engineers listen to Zo (The Scientist) more than attorneys do.
