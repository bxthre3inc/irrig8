# Strategy: Approaching the State (DWR & SEO)

To win with the State of Colorado, Bxthre3 must be positioned as a
**Verification Utility** that makes the State Engineer\'s job easier,
not harder.

## 1. The Target Agencies

-   **DWR (Division of Water Resources):** The \"Regulators.\"

-   **SEO (State Engineer\'s Office):** The \"Enforcers.\"

-   **CWCB (Colorado Water Conservation Board):** The \"Funders.\"

## 2. The \"Non-Threatening\" Position

The State is often wary of private data. We must approach them with the
**\"Open Ledger, Private Logic\"** model:

-   **The Message:** \"We aren\'t here to challenge your models; we are
    > here to provide the **Ground Truth** that confirms your
    > conservation goals are being met.\"

-   **The Utility:** Offer the DWR a \"State Auditor\" view of the **IRP
    > Ledger**. They don\'t see the farmer\'s proprietary \"Zo\" logic,
    > but they *do* see a cryptographically signed confirmation that \"X
    > acre-feet were saved on Parcel Y.\"

## 3. The \"SB-23\" or Grant Funding Hook

Colorado frequently releases funds for \"Water Speculation Prevention\"
and \"Measurement Accuracy.\"

-   **The Proposal:** Request a meeting with the **Division 3 Engineer**
    > to demonstrate how the **DEM Protocol** prevents \"unauthorized
    > takes\" during administrative shutdowns.

-   **The Pilot:** Propose a \"State-Sanctioned Validation Study\" where
    > Bxthre3 hardware is used to verify the accuracy of the State\'s
    > existing telemetry.

## 4. The Regulatory \"Standard-Setting\"

Our goal is to get the **QCV (Quantifiable Conservation Validation)**
listed as an \"Approved Method of Measurement\" in the State Engineer\'s
Rules.

-   **Process:** Once the June 29th Judge accepts Bxthre3 data as
    > evidence, we immediately file a \"Petition for Rulemaking\" to
    > have our **IRP Ledger** recognized as a standard for all future
    > groundwater reporting in the Rio Grande Basin.

## 5. Summary: From \"Company\" to \"Infrastructure\"

If the State adopts our **Standardized Technical Nomenclature** (e.g.,
calling it \"Immutable Resource Provenance\"), we have effectively won.
We become the \"Trusted Third Party\" between the Farmer and the State.
