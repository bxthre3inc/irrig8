## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The SLV Technology Commons (SLVTC) transforms Bxthre3 Inc's portfolio of ag-tech, gaming, AI, and climate ventures into a **physical economic engine** for Colorado's San Luis Valley. It combines:

- **Zone A (Nonprofit Commons):** Edge data center, co-working/makerspace, shared labs, equipment library
- **Zone B (For-Park):** Light industrial build-to-suit for venture manufacturing
- **Zone C (Demo Fields):** 40-acre living laboratory for precision agriculture research

**All powered by AgentOS, protected by 8+ patents, and designed to attract climate-tech startups globally.**

## III. GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

### SLVTC — 501(c)(3) Nonprofit Organization

**Board Composition (5 seats):**
- 2 seats: Bxthre3 Inc (appoints)
- 1 seat: Colorado State University (appoints)
- 1 seat: Local community representative (SLVDRG, ValleyWide, or similar)
- 1 seat: Independent (ag-tech/gaming industry expert)

**Voting:** 3/5 quorum, majority vote, Bxthre3 cannot unilaterally decide

**Key Committees:**
- **Investment Committee:** Reviews tenant applications, sets subsidy levels
- **Infrastructure Committee:** Maintains data center, equipment, facilities
- **Research Committee:** Oversees Zone C, CSU partnership, data access

---

### Bxthre3 Inc — Parent Organization

**Role:** Founder, initial funder, technology provider (AgentOS), anchor tenant (Irrig8)

**Financial Flow:**
```
Bxthre3 Inc → Donations/Grants → SLVTC (tax-deductible)
Bxthre3 Inc → Lease Payments → SLVTC (Zone B buildings)
Bxthre3 Inc → Licensing Fees → SLVTC (AgentOS tenant version)
SLVTC → FMV Leases → Bxthre3 subsidiaries (Irrig8, VPC, etc.)
```

**Arm's Length Requirements (IRS Safe Harbor):**
- All leases at Fair Market Value (appraisal required)
- All services at FMV (comparable market rates)
- No preferential treatment for Bxthre3 tenants in admissions
- Board majority independent for key decisions
