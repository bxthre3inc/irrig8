## Getting Started — 4 Steps

### Step 1: Select Your Partner Track

Contact your Channel Sales Director to determine your tier (Bronze / Silver / Gold) and agree on initial goals.

### Step 2: Sign Partner Agreement

Your Partner Agreement defines:
- Revenue share / margin structure
- Deal registration terms
- Exclusivity terms (if any)
- Term and termination

**Contact:** channels@bxthre3.com

### Step 3: Complete Deal Registration

For every deal you want to protect, submit a Deal Registration Form (see Appendix A). We respond within 2 business days with margin confirmation.

### Step 4: Begin Selling

Once registered, you'll receive:
- Access to partner portal (TBD)
- Sales playbooks and battle cards
- Product one-pagers for your customers
- Access to co-marketing budget (Silver + Gold)

## Key Policies

### Deal Registration
- Submit before the customer is contacted by a competitor
- 90-day protection window per registered deal
- Registration reviewed within 2 business days
- Protected margin locked at time of registration

### Exclusivity
- Regional exclusivity available for Gold tier partners
- Requires minimum deal targets and signed exclusivity addendum

### Payment Terms
- Quarterly commission payouts (end of Q + 30 days)
- Minimum payout threshold: $500
- Wire transfer fees absorbed by Bxthre3 for Gold partners
