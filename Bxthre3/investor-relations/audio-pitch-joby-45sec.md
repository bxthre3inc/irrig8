## DELIVERY NOTES

**Pace:** Conversational but confident — not rushed, not salesy  
**Tone:** "Smart friend explaining what he's building" — not infomercial  
**Emphasis points:**
- "doesn't hallucinate" — say it like it's a real differentiator (it is)
- "five million cap, thirty percent discount" — clear numbers, slight pause between
- "thirteen-week runway" — say this straight, no apology — honesty builds trust
- "disproportionate upside" — this is the hook, slight emphasis

**Natural pauses:** After "Hey Joby — it's BX3." [pause]  
**Voice memo format:** Works as voicemail, WhatsApp voice note, or recorded message
