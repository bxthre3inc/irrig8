## Negotiation Playbook

| Tactic | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| Annual pre-pay discount | 10–15% savings for annual commitment |
| Multi-year lock-in | Additional 5–10% for 2+ year terms |
| Volume commitments | Negotiate tiered pricing above thresholds |
| Most-favored-customer | Ensure best pricing if vendor discounts others |
| Benchmark leverage | Use competing quotes to negotiate down |
