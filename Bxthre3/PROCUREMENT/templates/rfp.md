## 4. Proposal Format

Proposals must include:

1. **Executive Summary** (1–2 pages)
2. **Technical Approach** — How you will meet the requirements
3. **Timeline & Milestones**
4. **Team & Experience** — Key personnel, relevant case studies
5. **Pricing** — Itemized breakdown, payment terms
6. **References** — At least 2 relevant client references
7. **Exceptions** — Any deviations from RFP requirements
8. **Appendices** — Supporting documentation
