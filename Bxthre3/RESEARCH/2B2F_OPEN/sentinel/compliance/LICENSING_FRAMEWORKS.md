### 2. Major Payment Institution (Singapore — MAS)

#### License Tiers

| Tier | Annual Volume Threshold | Capital Requirement | Scope |
|------|------------------------|---------------------|-------|
| **Standard** | <S$3M e-money | S$100K | Limited |
| **Major Payment Institution (MPI)** | ≥S$3M e-money | S$250K | Full |

#### Ongoing Requirements

| Requirement | Frequency | Details |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Capital Adequacy** | Monthly | Maintain liquid assets ≥ 100% of outstanding e-money |
| **Audit** | Annual | External auditor, MAS-prescribed scope |
| **Compliance Officer** | Continuous | At least one full-time compliance officer |
| **Technology Risk Management** | Annual | TRM audit, penetration testing |
| **Reporting** | Quarterly | Transaction volumes, suspicious activity |
| **License Fee** | Annual | S$13,600 (~$10K USD) |

#### Compliance Timeline

```
Month 1-2:  Entity formation, compliance framework setup
Month 3:    Application submission with all documentation
Month 4-8:  MAS review period (can extend to 12 months)
Month 9:    Conditional approval (if applicable)
Month 9-12: Final approval and license grant
```

### 3. Crypto Asset Service Provider (EU — MiCA)

#### CASP Categories

| Category | Activity Scope | Capital Requirement | Authorization Level |
|----------|---------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **Class 1** | Custody, exchange, broker | €150K | National authority |
| **Class 2** | Class 1 + proprietary trading | €350K | National authority |
| **Class 3** | All CASP activities | €350K | National + EBA oversight |

#### Ongoing Requirements (MiCA — Post-2024)

| Requirement | Frequency | Details |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Capital Maintenance** | Continuous | Minimum capital + own funds calculation |
| **Audit** | Annual | Statutory audit, MiCA-specific requirements |
| **Compliance Officer** | Continuous | Two control persons minimum |
| **Custody Arrangements** | Continuous | Segregated accounts, insurance |
| **White Paper** | One-time | Crypto-asset white paper (if applicable) |
| **Reporting** | Quarterly | EBA standardized reporting |

#### MiCA Timeline Challenge

**Critical Path Issue:** MiCA fully effective 2024-2025, but:
- National implementations vary in speed
- EBA regulatory technical standards (RTS) still being finalized
- First CASP authorizations may not be available until late 2025/2026

**Strategic Recommendation:** Defer EU market entry until CASP framework stabilizes (M15+)

### 6. Wyoming Special Purpose Depository Institution (SPDI)

#### Unique US Banking Charter

**Key Feature:** SPDI can custody digital assets in a banking framework.

| Requirement | Amount | Notes |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Minimum Capital** | $25M | Non-negotiable |
| **Liquid Assets** | 100% of deposits | Full reserve banking |
| **Audit** | Annual | State + federal oversight |
| **FDIC Insurance** | N/A | Not available — full reserve model |
| **Application Timeline** | 12-18 months | Lengthy process |
| **Compliance Officer** | Required | Bank-level compliance staff |

#### SPDI Strategic Value

- **Custody Legitimacy:** Banking charter provides highest regulatory clarity for digital asset custody
- **Fed Master Account:** Eligible (though not guaranteed) — would enable direct Fedwire access
- **Cost Trade-off:** High capital requirement ($25M) vs. maximum regulatory certainty

**Recommendation:** Consider for Phase 2/3 if custody becomes core revenue line; defer in Phase 1.
