# Licensing Frameworks for 2B2F Operations

> **SENTINEL Research — Day 1 Output**

> **Status:** Foundation Phase (v0.1)

## Overview

Preliminary mapping of required licenses for 2B2F entity operations across target jurisdictions. Covers financial services, money transmission, digital assets, and specialized services requiring regulatory approval.

## License Taxonomy

### Tier 1: Critical Path (Must Have)
| License | Purpose | Timeline | Complexity |
|---------|---------|----------|------------|
| Money Transmitter | Move value across networks | 12-24 months | High |
| Banking Charter | Custody and lending | 24-36 months | Very High |
| Securities Dealer | Asset trading/issuance | 12-18 months | High |
| Trust Charter | Fiduciary asset holding | 18-24 months | High |

### Tier 2: Expansion Enablers (Should Have)
| License | Purpose | Timeline | Complexity |
|---------|---------|----------|------------|
| Payment Institution | Multi-currency transfers | 6-12 months | Medium |
| E-Money | Stored value issuance | 8-14 months | Medium |
| Custody Provider | Safekeeping | 12-18 months | Medium-High |
| Asset Manager | Portfolio management | 18-24 months | High |

### Tier 3: Specialized (Phase 3+)
| License | Purpose | Timeline | Complexity |
|---------|---------|----------|------------|
| Insurance | Risk transfer | 24-48 months | Very High |
| Exchange Operator | Trading venue | 12-18 months | High |
| Derivatives | Contract issuance | 18-30 months | Very High |

## Jurisdiction-Specific Pathways

### United States (Federal + State)

#### Federal
| License | Agency | Timeline | Cost |
|---------|--------|----------|------|
| Bank Charter (National) | OCC | 18-36 months | $500K-$2M |
| Money Transmitter (MT) | FinCEN | 6-12 months | $15K-$50K |
| Trust Charter | OCC/OCC | 18-24 months | $75K-$200K |

| State | MT Timeline | Capital Requirements | Renewal |

## Critical Path Dependencies

1. **Month 0-6:** Legal entity + FinCEN registration (parallel)
2. **Month 6-18:** Money transmitter licenses (sequential states)
3. **Month 12-24:** Singapore MPI + Swiss Fintech (parallel)
4. **Month 18-36:** Banking charter + securities (converging)
5. **Month 24-48:** UAE + specialized (optional)

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

| Risk | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|------------|-------|
| License denial | Multi-jurisdiction applications | Legal |
| Capital shortfall | Phased raises tied to approvals | Finance |
| Timeline drift | Accelerated tracks (WY, SG, UAE) | Compliance |
| Revocation | Robust AML/KYC infrastructure | Operations |
