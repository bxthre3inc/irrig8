## Risk Framework Overview

Preliminary categorization of risk vectors across 120-month execution timeline. Risks are scored by probability (P) and impact (I), with aggregate risk scores (R = P × I) for prioritization.

Scoring Scale: 1-10 (Probability × Impact)
- Low: 1-25
- Medium: 26-50
- High: 51-75
- Critical: 76-100

## Scenario Planning: Stress Tests

### Scenario A: Regulatory Siege
- Trigger: Major government bans primary assets
- Impact: Operations forced offshore
- Probability: 3/10
- Response: Multi-jurisdiction redundancy activates

### Scenario B: Macro Collapse
- Trigger: 2008-style financial crisis
- Impact: Funding dried up, operations shrink
- Probability: 4/10
- Response: Low-burn hibernation mode

### Scenario C: Technology Disruption
- Trigger: Quantum computing breaks current crypto
- Impact: Asset class obsolescence
- Probability: 5/10
- Response: Post-quantum migration

### Scenario D: Black Swan
- Trigger: Unforeseen systemic event
- Impact: Existential threat
- Probability: 1/10
- Response: Maximal insurance/hedging
