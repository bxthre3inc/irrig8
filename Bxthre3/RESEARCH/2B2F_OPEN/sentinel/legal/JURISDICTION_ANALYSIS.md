## US State Analysis

### Delaware (Corp Standard)
**Pros:** Established case law, business-friendly courts, predictable
**Cons:** Tax obligations complex, requires registered agent, C-Corp weight
**Verdict:** Baseline standard, not optimal for 2B2F flexibility

### Wyoming (Innovation Friendly)
**Pros:** No corporate income tax, privacy protections, DAO legislation
**Cons:** Limited precedent, smaller talent pool
**Verdict:** Strong candidate for holding structures

### South Dakota (Asset Protection)
**Pros:** Trust statutes, creditor protections, dynasty trusts
**Cons:** Limited corporate infrastructure
**Verdict:** Subsidiary consideration for asset isolation

### Colorado (Home Base)
**Pros:** brodiblanco proximity, tech sector growth, water rights precedent
**Cons:** Higher taxes, less corporate law sophistication
**Verdict:** Operational hub potential
