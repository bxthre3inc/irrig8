# 2B2F 120-Month Execution Plan — Master Framework

> **SENTINEL Strategic Planning Unit**  
> **Lead Author:** brodiblanco (overwatch)  
> **Council Mandate:** 12-month research phase → 120-month execution  
> **GDP Targets:** 1% @ M24 | 5% @ M36 | 60%+ @ M120  

---

## Executive Summary

This document establishes the master framework for 2B2F ("Too Big To Fail") alternative economic system deployment across 120 months. The plan is organized into four phases, each with distinct objectives, regulatory milestones, and GDP impact targets.

**Core Design Principles:**
1. **Regulatory-First:** Compliance infrastructure precedes market expansion
2. **Jurisdictionally Diversified:** Multi-sovereign redundancy mitigates single-point-of-failure
3. **SIFI-Ready:** Design for systemic importance from inception
4. **Zero-Budget Research:** Public methodology, transparent governance
