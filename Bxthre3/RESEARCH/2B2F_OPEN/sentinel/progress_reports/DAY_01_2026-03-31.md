### 3. Critical Compliance Insight

European Central Bank working paper (March 2026) establishes key test for decentralization:
- **No controlling legal entity**
- **No concentrated control** over service
- **Decentralization across entire value chain** (smart contracts + governance + operation)

Failure on any dimension = subject to existing financial regulations.

### 4. UNCITRAL Model Law on Automated Contracting (MLAC)

Framework for legally enforceable smart contracts:
- Offer/acceptance via blockchain
- Capacity and consideration requirements
- Attribution of responsibility challenges
- Jurisdiction determination unresolved

**Implication for 2B2F:** Smart contract architecture must incorporate legal compliance at design phase, not retrofit.
