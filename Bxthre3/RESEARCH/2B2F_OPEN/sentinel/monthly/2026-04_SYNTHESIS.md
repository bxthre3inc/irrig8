## Key Research Findings: Week 1

### 1. SEC No-Action Precedent: Critical Window

**DoubleZero Foundation (Sep 29, 2025)** and **Fuse Energy Token (2025)** establish clear precedent for utility token structures:

| Precedent Element | DoubleZero | Fuse | 2B2F Application |
|-------------------|------------|------|------------------|
| Token type | DePIN network reward | Energy trading utility | TBD (operational reward likely) |
| Consumptive use | ✓ Yes (network fees) | ✓ Yes (energy consumption) | Must design for consumptive |
| Profit expectation | ✗ No marketing | ✗ No marketing | Critical: no yield promises |
| Decentralized governance | ✓ Yes | ✓ Yes | Required from inception |
| SEC relief | Granted | Granted | Template for application |

**Strategic Implication:** 2B2F token design must mirror these precedents — operational rewards for network participation, not investment contracts.

### 2. CFTC Thresholds: Scaling Risk Vector

| Threshold | Trigger | 2B2F Risk @ M120 |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| $8B AANA | Swap dealer registration | High (if synthetic instruments) |
| Initial margin | Uncleared swaps >$8B | High (if leverage offered) |
| De minimis exemption | Complex calculation | Avoid dependency |

**Mitigation:** Design token as pure utility/commodity (not swap), avoid leverage mechanics.

### 3. FSOC SIFI Designation: Long-Term Certainty

Current FSOC guidance (2026) reinstitutes "very high bar" for nonbank SIFI designation:
- No designations since MetLife (2014)
- Activities-based approach preferred
- 180-day off-ramp for mitigation

**Scaling Trajectory:**
| GDP Impact | SIFI Risk | Action |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| <1% (M24) | Negligible | Standard compliance |
| 1-5% (M36) | Low | Monitor, prepare |
| 5-20% (M60-84) | Medium | Engage FSOC proactively |
| 20-60% (M96-108) | High | SIFI compliance design |
| >60% (M120) | **Certain** | Full SIFI infrastructure |

**Strategic Implication:** Design for SIFI compliance from inception — Phase 1 architecture must support Phase 4 requirements.

### 4. "Project Crypto" Regulatory Window

SEC Chair Paul Atkins announced **Project Crypto** (July 2025):
- Innovation exemption regime (principles-based)
- Framework for tokenized securities
- Modernized custody rules
- "Reg Super-App" for broker-dealers

**Timing:** 12-month planning phase (M1-M12) coincides with potential regulatory modernization. Early SEC engagement in Months 1-6 may secure favorable positioning.
