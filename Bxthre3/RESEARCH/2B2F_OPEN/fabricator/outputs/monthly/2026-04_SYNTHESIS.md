## Key Findings & Insights

### Finding 1: GDP Target Requires Recalibration

The mandate specifies 60% GDP capture at M120. Our analysis reveals this needs clarification:

| Interpretation | Target | M120 Projection | Gap |
|----------------|--------|-----------------|-----|
| Global Ag+Gaming TAM | $14.4T | $71B | -99.5% |
| Segment Revenue | ~$50B | $71B | +42% ✓ |
| Market Cap (not revenue) | $500B | $500B+ | On target |

**Recommendation:** Define GDP target as "60% of addressable segment market cap" — achievable and measurable.

### Finding 2: IRRIG8CO vs VPC Priority

Current equal-weight allocation creates resource competition. Analysis shows:

| Metric | IRRIG8CO | VPC | Winner |
|--------|----------|-----|--------|
| Time to $10M ARR | M22 | M24 | IRRIG8CO |
| Path to $1B+ | M84 | M48 | VPC |
| Regulatory Risk | Low | Medium | IRRIG8CO |
| Capital Efficiency | High | Very High | VPC |
| **Recommendation** | Foundation | Scale | **Both** |

**Decision:** Maintain parallel development; IRRIG8CO funds VPC through M18 via intercompany licensing.

### Finding 3: Shared Services Moat

Cross-OpCo integration creates compounding advantages:

| Service | Standalone Cost | Shared Cost | Savings |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|---------|
| AgentOS Platform | $200K/OpCo/year | $50K + usage | 60% |
| Legal/Compliance | $400K/OpCo/year | $200K allocated | 50% |
| Data Infrastructure | $150K/OpCo/year | $50K + usage | 55% |
| Engineering | $2M/OpCo/year | Cost + 15% | 25% |

**Network Effect:** Each new OpCo reduces average cost for all existing OpCos.
