# 2B2F Stress-Test Framework

**Model ID:** 2B2F-STRESS-v0.1

## Executive Summary

This framework defines 8 stress scenarios for the 2B2F 120-month execution plan. Each scenario includes probability assessment, financial impact at key milestones, early warning indicators, and response protocols.

---

## Scenario Matrix

### 1. Adoption Lag Scenario
**Probability:** 20% | **Severity:** High

**Trigger:** Customer acquisition 50% slower than projected due to market education requirements, longer sales cycles, or product-market fit gaps.

**Financial Impact:**

| Milestone | Baseline | Stress | Delta |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| M24 Revenue | $59.4M | $18M | -70% |
| M36 Revenue | $702M | $210M | -70% |
| M120 Revenue | $71.1B | $28B | -61% |

**Early Warning Indicators:**
- CAC exceeds $1,000 by M12 (vs $800 target)
- Conversion rate <2% from trial to paid
- Sales cycle >90 days (vs 45-day target)

**Response Protocol:**
1. **M6:** Pivot to channel partnerships (co-op extension services)
2. **M12:** Free-forever tier with premium upsell
3. **M18:** Strategic acquisition of competitor with installed base

## Mitigation Strategies (Cross-Scenario)

### Portfolio Diversification
- Multi-OpCo structure reduces single-point-of-failure
- IRRIG8CO (B2B) + VPC (B2C) correlation <0.5
- Geographic diversification (planned M36+)

### Capital Structure Resilience
- Maintain 18-month runway at all times
- Undrawn credit facility: $5M
- Convertible note structure preserves early investor protection

### Operational Flexibility
- Modular architecture enables OpCo independence
- Cloud-native infrastructure (no CapEx lock-in)
- Contractor-heavy model (65% of workforce) for scaling flexibility
