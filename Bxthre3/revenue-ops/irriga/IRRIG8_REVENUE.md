## Revenue Recognition (ASC 606)

### Hardware — Point-in-Time
- **Trigger:** Delivery confirmation (proof of delivery signed)
- **Recognition:** Full ASP recorded as revenue on delivery date
- **Deferred:** 0 (hardware has no remaining performance obligation)

### SaaS — Ratably Over Time
- **Trigger:** Time elapsed (daily/weekly/monthly)
- **Recognition:** ACV ÷ 365 × days elapsed
- **Deferred:** Unbilled portion of annual contracts

### Pilot Programs — Percentage of Completion
- **Trigger:** Milestone achievement (per SOW)
- **Method:** Input method (costs incurred vs. total expected)
- **Deferred:** Undelivered milestone value

### Grant-funded — As Expended
- **Trigger:** Expenditure incurrence (per grant terms)
- **Method:** Cost reimbursement or fixed award
- **Deferred:** Unspent grant balance
