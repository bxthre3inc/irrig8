## Bull Case (+30% vs Base)

### Revenue Drivers
- Early pilot success — 1,600 fields vs. 1,280 target
- Enterprise customer closes early (+$500K)
- Hardware upsell to existing customers (+$500K)
- Higher ASP due to Plus/Enterprise tier mix

### Bull Case P&L

| Line Item | Bull | Base | Delta |
|-----------|------|------|-------|
| Total Revenue | $9,100,000 | $7,000,000 | +$2,100,000 |
| Total COR | ($3,276,000) | ($4,456,000) | +$1,180,000 |
| Gross Profit | $5,824,000 | $2,544,000 | +$3,280,000 |
| Gross Margin % | 64.0% | 36.3% | +27.7pp |
| Total OpEx | ($5,200,000) | ($5,200,000) | $0 |
| **EBITDA** | **$624,000** | **($2,656,000)** | **+$3,280,000** |
| **EBITDA Margin %** | **6.9%** | **-37.9%** | **+44.8pp** |

### Key Assumptions
- Pilot conversion rate: 100% (all 1,280 fields live by Q4)
- Enterprise deal: +$500K in Q3
- Hardware bundle upsell: 50% of customers add SFD-28 kit
- Gross margin improves to 64% (hardware mix shift)

---

## Bear Case (-40% vs Base)

### Revenue Drivers
- Pilot delays — only 768 fields live by Q4 (60% of target)
- Lower ASP due to Base tier mix (more farmers than enterprises)
- Hardware revenue delayed (customers want to see SaaS working first)
- No enterprise deal this year

### Bear Case P&L

| Line Item | Bear | Base | Delta |
|-----------|------|------|-------|
| Total Revenue | $4,200,000 | $7,000,000 | ($2,800,000) |
| Total COR | ($2,940,000) | ($4,456,000) | +$1,516,000 |
| Gross Profit | $1,260,000 | $2,544,000 | ($1,284,000) |
| Gross Margin % | 30.0% | 36.3% | -6.3pp |
| Total OpEx | ($5,516,000) | ($5,200,000) | ($316,000) |
| **EBITDA** | **($4,256,000)** | **($2,656,000)** | **($1,600,000)** |
| **EBITDA Margin %** | **-101.3%** | **-37.9%** | **-63.4pp** |

### Key Assumptions
- Pilot conversion rate: 60% (768 fields by Q4)
- Enterprise deal: $0 (delayed to 2027)
- Hardware revenue: 40% below plan
- OpEx increase: +$316K (extended field support costs)
