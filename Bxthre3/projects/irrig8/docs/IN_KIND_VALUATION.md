# PART I: REPLACEMENT COST METHODOLOGY

## 1.1 Why Replacement Cost?

Grant agencies and investors accept **in-kind contributions** as founder "skin in the game." The standard is **replacement cost** — what it would cost to hire professionals to do equivalent work.
