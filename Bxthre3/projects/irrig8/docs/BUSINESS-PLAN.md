# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## The Deterministic Farming Operating System

FarmSense is an end-to-end precision agriculture platform that replaces stochastic, intuition-based irrigation with data-driven deterministic optimization. By deploying a multi-layered sensor network across the Soil-Plant-Atmosphere Continuum (SPAC), FarmSense achieves 20-30% water savings while increasing crop ROI by 18-22%.

## The Economic Catalyst: Water Scarcity as Market Driver

The San Luis Valley (SLV) faces an existential hydro-economic crisis:
- 89,000 acre-foot annual aquifer depletion
- $500/acre-foot groundwater pumping fee (4× increase from 2023)
- $25,200 annual savings per 126-acre pivot at 20% water reduction
- Legal necessity: Water Court admissibility via Digital Water Ledger

## Investment Ask

**Seed Round: $3.5M** (Q2 2026)
- $1.5M Hardware manufacturing (1,280 units for SLV Subdistrict 1)
- $1.0M Engineering (12 FTE expansion)
- $0.5M Field operations (deployment & support)
- $0.5M Working capital & compliance

**Use of Proceeds:** Full deployment of SLV Subdistrict 1 (1,280 fields, 300,000 acres) by Q4 2026.
