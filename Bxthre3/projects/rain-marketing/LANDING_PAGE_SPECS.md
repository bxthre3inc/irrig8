## FAQ Section

**Q: Is this really free? What's the catch?**
A: No catch. The free report demonstrates RAIN's value. We hope you'll consider the full platform, but there's no obligation.

**Q: How current is the data?**
A: We refresh regulatory databases daily. Your report reflects filings through yesterday.

**Q: What basins do you cover?**
A: Currently: Rio Grande (CO/NM), Arkansas (CO), South Platte (CO), and Colorado River headwaters. Expanding based on demand.

**Q: Who has access to my information?**
A: Only RAIN's research team. We do not share contact information with third parties without explicit consent.

**Q: How is this different from just checking state water court filings?**
A: RAIN monitors 47 distinct data sources including federal, tribal, county, and non-public lease applications—most of which never appear in water court records until too late.
