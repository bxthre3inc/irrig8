# AgentOS Business Builder Contracts

## Overview

Standard contract templates for AgentOS Business Builder engagements.

**Note:** Have an attorney review all contracts before use. These are starting templates, not legal advice.

---

## Available Templates

### 1. Service Agreement
**File:** `SERVICE_AGREEMENT.md`

Covers:
- Scope of work
- Deliverables per tier
- Payment terms
- Timeline
- Revision limits

### 2. Non-Disclosure Agreement (Mutual)
**File:** `NDA.md`

Covers:
- Mutual confidentiality
- Term and termination
- Exclusions

### 3. Statement of Work (SOW)
**File:** `SOW.md`

Covers:
- Project-specific details
- Milestones and deliverables
- Acceptance criteria

1. Choose template based on engagement type
2. Customize with specific details
