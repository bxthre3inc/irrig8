## Customization Guide

### Before Customizing

1. Replace Bxthre3 branding with your agency name
2. Update contact information
3. Add your own case studies (if available)
4. Adjust pricing if offering different terms

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Dark navy (#1a1a2e)
- Accent: Electric blue (#00d4ff)
- Text: White on dark, dark gray on light

---

## Usage Tips

- Lead with Starter tier to lower barrier
- Upsell to Professional for full package
- Enterprise for custom builds and white-label
- Always offer free discovery call first
