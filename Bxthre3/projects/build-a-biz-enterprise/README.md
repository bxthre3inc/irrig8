## Tier Descriptions

### Basic — $997
For solo founders and first-time AI adopters. Includes 3 core agents, basic playbook, and 30-day support.

### Professional — $2,500
For small teams (2-5). Includes 5 agents, complete playbook library, contract templates, and 90-day support.

### Ultimate — $5,000
For growing agencies. Includes 8 agents, advanced playbooks, white-label prep, and 6-month support.

### Enterprise — Custom ($10K+)
For established businesses. Includes 10+ custom agents, white-label, dedicated support, and quarterly strategy calls.
