## WHAT BUILD-A-BIZ ACTUALLY DOES

### Layer 1: The Inbox (AI Triages Everything)

| What Happens | AI Action | Owner Sees |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Voicemail left | Transcribed, categorized | "New estimate request — Toyota Camry, rear bumper" |
| Email inquiry | Drafted response with quote template | "Approve quote: $485? Y/N" |
| Facebook message | Instant reply + appointment booking | "Booked: Thu 9am, added to calendar" |
| Missed call | Text sent: "Sorry we missed you! Reply ESTIMATE for quick quote" | "Lead captured, follow-up scheduled" |

### Layer 2: Marketing (AI Runs the Brand)

| Task | AI Does | Owner Does |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Before/after photos | Auto-captured, edited, watermarked | Nothing |
| Instagram posts | Written, scheduled, posted 3x/week | Nothing |
| Google reviews | Monitored, responded to (all 5 stars get thanks, complaints escalated) | Apologize if 1-star (rare) |
| Local SEO | Google Business optimized, keywords tracked | Nothing |
| Facebook ads | $10/day budget managed, A/B tested | Nothing |

### Layer 3: Operations (AI Runs the Business)

| Task | AI Does | Owner Does |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Invoicing | Auto-generated on completion | Nothing |
| Follow-ups | "Hi Mike, how's the paint holding up?" 2 weeks later | Nothing |
| Scheduling | Optimized route, buffer time added | Show up |
| Parts ordering | Inventory tracked, low-stock alerts, suppliers auto-called | Approve $200+ orders |
| Payroll | Hours tracked (if employees), calculations ready | Review & approve |
