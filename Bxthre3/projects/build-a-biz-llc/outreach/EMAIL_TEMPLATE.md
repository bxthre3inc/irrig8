# Cold Email Template - Small Business Outreach

**Subject Line Options:**
- Quick question about [BUSINESS NAME]
- Helping [TOWN] businesses grow online
- Free digital audit for [BUSINESS NAME]

---

**Email Body:**

Hi [CONTACT NAME],

I'm [YOUR NAME] with Valley Build-A-Biz LLC — we help small businesses in the San Luis Valley and across Colorado get found online and run more efficiently.

I noticed [BUSINESS NAME] doesn't have a website yet, and that's costing you customers every day. Most people search Google before they ever call or visit.

We offer:
- **Professional websites** — $500 one-time, $20/mo hosting
- **Google Business Profile optimization** — so you show up in local searches
- **Mobile apps** — custom apps for orders, appointments, customer management

I'd love to offer you a **free 15-minute call** to chat about what's holding your business back online. No pressure, no sales pitch — just a conversation.

Would you have time this week to talk?

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
Valley Build-A-Biz LLC
[PHONE NUMBER]

---

**Personalization Tips:**
- Mention something specific about their business ("Love your 5-star reviews on Google!")
- Reference their town ("Seen you guys doing great work in Monte Vista")
- Offer something of value ("Happy to share a free competitive audit")

**Best Send Times:**
- Tuesday-Thursday mornings (7-9 AM)
- Wednesday afternoon (2-4 PM)

**Follow-Up Sequence:**
1. Initial email (Day 1)
2. Follow-up (Day 4)
3. Final follow-up (Day 7)
4. Break - wait 30 days before next campaign
