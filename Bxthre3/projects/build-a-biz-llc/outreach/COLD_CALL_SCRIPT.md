# Cold Call Script

**Pre-Call Prep:**
- Know the business: name, location, industry, reviews
- Have a website/app demo ready to share
- Keep script nearby but sound natural

---

## Opening (Aim for 10-15 seconds)

"Hi, is this [CONTACT NAME]? Hi, this is [YOUR NAME] with Valley Build-A-Biz LLC. Do you have 30 seconds? I help local businesses in [TOWN] get websites and apps that bring in more customers. I noticed [BUSINESS NAME] doesn't have a website yet, and I wanted to see if that's something you'd be interested in exploring."

**If they say YES:** Continue to Discovery  
**If they say NO/HANG UP:** "No problem, thanks for your time. Have a great day!"  

---

## Discovery Questions

1. "How long have you been in business?"
2. "How do most of your new customers find you right now?"
3. "Do you have any online presence — website, Google listing, social media?"
4. "What's been the biggest challenge with getting more customers?"

**Listen for pain points:**
- No online presence
- Relying on word-of-mouth
- Competitors with better websites
- Too busy to handle marketing

---

## Value Pitch

"Here's what I typically tell business owners: Most customers Google a business before they call or visit. If you're not showing up, you're invisible to them. We build professional websites starting at $500, and we handle everything — hosting, setup, even Google Business optimization."

**Key Differentiators to Mention:**
- Local — we're based in the Valley, we understand the market
- All-in-one — website, apps, Google, social — one partner
- Affordable — $500 one-time, $20/mo — no hidden fees

---

## Handle Objections

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I already have a website" | "Great! Is it showing up in Google? Would you like a free audit to see what's working and what isn't?" |
| "I'm too busy" | "Totally understand. That's why we handle everything — you just answer a few questions and we build it. Takes about a week." |
| "I can't afford it" | "I hear you. Our websites start at $500, and we also offer payment plans. What's your timeline for bringing in more customers?" |
| "Send me an email" | "Absolutely. What's your email? I'll send over our info and some examples of businesses like yours. Then we can follow up in a few days." |

---

## Close Options

**Option A — Schedule Call:**
"Would you have 15 minutes Thursday or Friday for a more detailed chat? I can show you examples and walk through exactly what we'd build."

**Option B — Send Info:**
"I'll email you our brochure and some local examples. When's a good time to follow up — early next week?"

**Option C — Demo:**
"Can I show you a quick 2-minute demo of what we build? I can text you a link right now."

---

## Post-Call Actions

1. **Log outcome** in lead tracker immediately
2. **Send follow-up email/text** within 2 hours if promised
3. **Schedule follow-up** if interest expressed
4. **Note objections** to refine pitch

---

## Quick Reference — Key Stats to Drop in Conversation

- "97% of customers search online before visiting"
- "We've helped 60+ businesses in the Valley"
- "Websites typically pay for themselves in 3-6 months"
- "Google Business Profile can increase calls by 50%"
