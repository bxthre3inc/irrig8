# Cold SMS Template

**Message:**

Hi [CONTACT NAME]! 👋 This is [YOUR NAME] from Valley Build-A-Biz LLC. We help local businesses in [TOWN] get websites & apps that bring in more customers. Quick question — would you be open to a 2-min call this week to see if we could help? No pressure either way. Reply YES or just give me a call at [PHONE NUMBER] - thanks!

---

**Alternative (shorter):**

Hi [CONTACT NAME] — question for you. Do you have a website for [BUSINESS NAME]? If not, I'd love to show you how easy it is. Reply and I'll send you some examples from businesses like yours in the Valley.

---

**Rules:**
- ALWAYS include opt-out: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- Send between 10 AM - 7 PM local time
- Wait 3+ days between messages
- Never mention pricing in first message
- Use their business name

**Follow-Up Sequence:**
1. Initial message (Day 1)
2. Check-in (Day 4)
3. Break - wait 21 days
