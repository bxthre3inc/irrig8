## Business Profile

Whats The Dilla is a trendy quesadilla-focused restaurant in Pueblo with a strong brand identity and creative menu offerings. Located on Walking Stick Blvd, they specialize in "hearty, home-cooked meals" with a quesadilla twist. The restaurant has built a loyal following with its welcoming atmosphere, great music, and attentive staff.

### Key Differentiators
- **4.6-star rating** — above our 4.5+ threshold
- **Trendy branding** — strong aesthetic and social presence
- **Unique concept** — quesadilla-focused (differentiated from standard Mexican)
- **Multi-platform presence** — DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub
- **Active social media** — Facebook presence with regular updates

## Pain Points Identified

1. **Triple Platform Management** — managing 3 different tablet systems
2. **Brand Dilution** — trendy concept buried under DoorDash/Uber Eats generic branding
3. **Commission Erosion** — losing $500-1,000/month to platforms
4. **Customer Data Gap** — no way to market directly to repeat quesadilla lovers

---

## Value Proposition

- **Save $4,000-9,500/year** in commissions
- **Brand consistency** — app matches their trendy aesthetic
- **Loyalty program** — "10th quesadilla free" drives repeat business
- **Direct customer relationships** — own the customer data

---

## Outreach Hooks

1. **Brand Angle:** "Your concept is too cool to be buried under DoorDash branding. Your own app keeps the experience on-brand."

2. **Triple Platform Pain:** "Three tablets, three commission structures, three companies taking your money. One app replaces them all."
