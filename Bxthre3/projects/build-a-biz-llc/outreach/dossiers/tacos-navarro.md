## Business Profile

Tacos Navarro is a beloved street taco spot in Pueblo known for authentic carne asada tacos. Reviewers call it "the best tacos I've ever had in Pueblo" and "best street tacos in Pueblo." Cash-only at location means customers would LOVE app ordering convenience.

### Key Differentiators
- **4.6-star rating** — strong reputation
- **"Go-to spot for carne asada street tacos"** — local legend status
- **Cash-only at location** — customers want digital payment option
- **Multiple locations** — 4 locations across Pueblo
- **Lunch-only** (11 AM - 3 PM) — limited hours = focused demand
