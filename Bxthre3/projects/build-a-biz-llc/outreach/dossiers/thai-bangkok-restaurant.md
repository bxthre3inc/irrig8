## Pain Points Identified

1. **Dual Platform Management** — managing orders across DoorDash AND Uber Eats creates operational friction
2. **Commission Erosion** — 25% of every sushi order goes to platforms (sushi = high margin, so high absolute loss)
3. **Small Town, Limited Options** — customers drive from across San Luis Valley; app would capture these destination diners
4. **No Customer Data** — platforms own the customer relationship, not the restaurant
