## Business Profile

Brues Alehouse Brewing Company is a premier brewpub located on the Historic Arkansas Riverwalk in Pueblo. Founded in 2015, they offer fresh craft beer, from-scratch food, and live music. Housed in a converted jailhouse with a biergarten, they're a destination dining experience.

### Key Differentiators
- **Riverwalk location** — prime tourism spot
- **Brewery + food** — craft beer drives food sales
- **Live music venue** — event space adds revenue
- **Converted jailhouse** — unique atmosphere
- **In-house bakery** — macarons, tarts, desserts
- **Event coordinator on staff** — Kaci Cambo

## Pain Points Identified

1. **Brewery = Different Model** — food supports beer, commission math different
2. **Event Complexity** — managing food for events + delivery = operational challenge
3. **Tourism Seasonality** — Riverwalk = busy summers, slow winters
4. **App Opportunity** — could integrate beer ordering, event tickets, food

---

## Value Proposition (Unique)

- **Beyond delivery** — app could include beer sales, event tickets, loyalty
- **Save $2,900-12,600/year** on food delivery commissions
- **Integrated experience** — one app for beer, food, events
- **Tourist capture** — visitors download app, become regulars
