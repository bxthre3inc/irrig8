## Business Profile

Presley's (also known as "The Lunchbox") is a highly-rated burger restaurant in Pueblo with exceptional customer reviews. - **4.9-star rating** — one of the highest-rated restaurants in Pueblo
- **Owner-named business** — Presley is the face of the brand
- **Best burger reputation** — strong word-of-mouth in southern Colorado
- **Great ambiance** — customers praise the atmosphere and service

## Pain Points Identified

1. **High Volume = High Commission Pain** — popular burgers mean lots of orders, each costing 25%
2. **Repeat Business** — burger customers reorder frequently; losing customer data means losing repeat marketing
3. **Competition** — Pueblo has many burger options; owning the customer relationship is critical
4. **Owner-Operated** — Presley likely feels every dollar lost to commissions personally
