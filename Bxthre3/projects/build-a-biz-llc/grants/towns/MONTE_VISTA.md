## Grant Access Strategy for Monte Vista

**Primary grants:**
1. Amber Grant — $10K/mo (women, rolling)
2. NASE Growth — $4K/qtr (any, quarterly)
3. USDA REAP — Up to $1M for energy/efficiency (farms/rural biz)
4. CO Advanced Industries — $50K-$250K (tech/manufacturing)
5. CO Startup Loan — $5K-$500K (state-backed)

**Support system:**
- CO SBDC (Southern Region) — free counseling
- Upper Rio Grande Economic Development — local resources
- Rio Grande County — permits, zoning support
