## Business Description

### What We Do

Valley Build-A-Biz LLC is a virtual operations and tech services company serving rural and underserved small businesses across Colorado's San Luis Valley and beyond. We help local entrepreneurs level the playing field by providing:

- **Website creation & hosting** — $500 one-time + $20/mo
- **Mobile app templates** — $500 one-time + $20-50/mo  
- **Google Business Profile optimization**
- **Social media content & posting**
- **Review management**
- **Admin operations support**

### Who We Serve

- Rural and underserved women-owned small businesses
- Local small businesses in the San Luis Valley
- Any small business needing tech + ops support

### Our Mission

To bring enterprise-grade digital tools to businesses that traditionally lack access to technology resources — making it possible for rural main street businesses to compete in today's digital economy.

## Crowdfunding Supplement (Optional)

IFundWomen has a **crowdfunding component** — you can raise additional funds from your network.

### Tips for Crowdfunding:

1. **Set a realistic goal** — $2,500-$5,000 matches well with grant
2. **Create a compelling campaign page** — Tell your story with photos/video
3. **Offer stretch rewards** — Even small donations get a thank-you
4. **Promote heavily** — Social media, email, community groups
5. **Update regularly** — Post progress, thank donors, share milestones

### Crowdfunding Timeline:
- Week 1: Launch campaign
- Week 2-3: Push hard on promotion
- Week 4: Final push + thank donors
