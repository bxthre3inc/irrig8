## Business Description

Valley Build-A-Biz LLC is a virtual operations and tech services company serving rural and underserved small businesses across Colorado's San Luis Valley and beyond. We help local entrepreneurs level the playing field against bigger competitors by providing professional websites, mobile applications, Google Business optimization, and marketing services at affordable, transparent pricing.

Our mission is to bring enterprise-grade digital tools to businesses that traditionally lack access to technology resources — making it possible for rural main street businesses to compete in today's digital economy.

**Services offered:**
- Website creation & hosting ($500 one-time + $20/mo)
- Mobile app templates ($500 one-time + $20-50/mo)
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Social media content & posting
- Review management & reputation monitoring
- Admin operations support (invoicing, scheduling, communications)

Rural small businesses in the San Luis Valley face a critical digital divide. While urban businesses have easy access to web developers, marketing agencies, and technology consultants, Valley businesses — particularly women-owned operations — are often left behind. Many have never had a website. Others rely entirely on word-of-mouth and Facebook. In a world where 97% of customers search online before visiting a business, this invisibility translates directly to lost revenue.

Valley Build-A-Biz exists to close this gap.

## Tips for AMBER Grant

### Timing Matters
- **Apply within the first 3 days of the month** — grants are awarded monthly and early applications have higher approval rates
- The AMBER grant awards $10,000 to one woman-owned business each month

### Storytelling Formula
1. **Hook:** Personal connection to the problem
2. **Journey:** Why you, why now
3. **Impact:** What you've done + what you'll do with the grant
4. **Vision:** Big picture, 2-3 years out

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Vague use of funds ("for business growth") → Be specific
- No personal story → Connect your background to the mission
- Focusing only on yourself → Emphasize community impact
- Waiting until the deadline → Apply early

### Keywords to Include
- Women-owned business
- Rural / underserved
- Community impact
- Economic development
- Job creation
