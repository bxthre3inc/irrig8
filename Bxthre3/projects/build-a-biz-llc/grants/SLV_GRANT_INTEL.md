## SLV TOWN MAPPING

### Monte Vista
**Demographics:** ~4,500, agriculture-heavy, county seat
**Economy:** Farming, some tourism, government services
**Niche opportunities:** Ag support, tourism services, food processing, tech for farms

### Center
**Demographics:** ~2,000, rural, agriculture-centered
**Economy:** Potato farming, trucking, small retail
**Niche opportunities:** Logistics, ag equipment, rural services, maintenance

### Alamosa
**Demographics:** ~9,500, college town (Adams State University)
**Economy:** Education, healthcare, retail, government
**Niche opportunities:** Student services, health/wellness, tech, education support

### Antonito
**Demographics:** ~750, historic railroad town
**Economy:** Tourism, Cumbres & Toltec Scenic Railroad, some ranching
**Niche opportunities:** Tourism services, hospitality, cultural experiences, art

## GRANT STACKING STRATEGY

**For women in SLV starting business:**
1. Apply for Amber Grant ($10,000) — rolling monthly
2. Apply NASE Growth Grant ($4,000) — quarterly
3. Connect with CO SBDC (free counseling + grant navigation)
4. If ag-related: USDA REAP for energy projects ($20K+)
5. If rural tech: Colorado Advanced Industries grant ($50K+)

**Bootstrap sequence:**
- Week 1: Form LLC ($50 CO SOS fee)
- Week 2: Get EIN (free IRS)
- Week 3: Open business checking
- Week 4: Submit Amber Grant app
- Week 8: Submit NASE Growth Grant
- Week 12: Close first client while grants processing
