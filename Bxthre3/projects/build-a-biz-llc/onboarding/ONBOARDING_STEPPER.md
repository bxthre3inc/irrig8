## PHASE 1: LLC FORMATION
### Week 1

**Goal:** Legally form Valley Build-A-Biz LLC in Colorado.

#### Step 1.1: Check Name Availability

- **Action:** Go to Colorado Secretary of State
- **Where to go:** https://sos.state.co.us/biz
- **What to do:**
  1. Look for "Business Entity Search" or "Name Availability Check"
  2. Enter "Valley Build-A-Biz LLC" in the search box
  3. Click Search
- **What to expect:** Results showing if the name is available or already taken
- **Troubleshooting:** If "Valley Build-A-Biz LLC" is taken, proceed to Step 1.2 with the backup name

#### Step 1.2: File Articles of Organization (If name available)

- **Action:** File your LLC formation documents
- **Where to go:** https://sos.state.co.us/biz → "File a New Entity"
- **What to do:**
  1. Select "Limited Liability Company (LLC)" as entity type
  2. Enter name: "Valley Build-A-Biz LLC" (or backup: "Build-A-Biz LLC")
  3. Enter principal office address (your address or registered agent address)
  4. Designate a registered agent (you can be your own agent in Colorado)
  5. Check the box: "These articles are signed by an authorized organizer"
- **What to expect:** A confirmation page and instruction to pay the filing fee

#### Step 1.3: Pay Filing Fee

- **Action:** Pay the $50 filing fee
- **Where to go:** Payment page on Colorado SOS website
- **What to do:**
  1. Enter credit/debit card information
  2. Confirm payment of $50
- **What to expect:** A confirmation receipt and your filing date
- **Troubleshooting:** If payment fails, try a different card or check with your bank

#### Step 1.4: Download and Save Confirmation

- **Action:** Save your filing confirmation
- **Where to go:** Your confirmation email or the SOS website
- **What to do:**
  1. Download the PDF of your Articles of Organization
  2. Save it in a safe folder: `/Documents/ValleyBuildABiz/legal/`
  3. Print a copy for your records
- **What to expect:** A confirmation document with your filing date and entity number
- **Troubleshooting:** If you don't receive an email within 1 hour, check spam folder or log back into the SOS portal

## PHASE 2: FEDERAL EIN
### Day 2-3

**Goal:** Obtain your Federal Employer Identification Number from the IRS.

#### Step 2.1: Apply for EIN Online

- **Action:** Get your EIN from the IRS
- **Where to go:** https://www.irs.gov/ein
- **What to do:**
  1. Click "Apply Online Now"
  2. Click "Start Application"
  3. Answer the questions:
     - **Entity type:** Limited Liability Company
     - **State where LLC was formed:** Colorado
     - **Reason for applying:** Starting a new business
     - **Owners/Principals:** Enter your name and SSN (last 4 digits for verification)
     - **Business name:** Valley Build-A-Biz LLC
     - **Business address:** Your address
     - **Activity:** Other consulting (code 541600)
  4. Submit the application
- **What to expect:** An EIN is generated immediately

#### Step 2.2: Download and Save EIN Letter

- **Action:** Save your EIN confirmation
- **Where to go:** The results page after applying
- **What to do:**
  1. Download/Print the EIN letter (CP 575)
  2. Save it in `/Documents/ValleyBuildABiz/legal/`
  3. Write your EIN on your intake form and tracker
- **What to expect:** A PDF showing your EIN (format: XX-XXXXXXX)
- **Troubleshooting:** If the online form doesn't work, call 1-800-829-4933 during business hours

## PHASE 3: BUSINESS BANK ACCOUNT
### Week 1-2

**Goal:** Open a separate business bank account for Valley Build-A-Biz LLC.

#### Step 3.1: Gather Required Documents

- **Action:** Collect your incorporation documents
- **What to bring:**
  1. EIN letter (from Phase 2)
  2. Articles of Organization (from Phase 1)
  3. Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport)
- **Tip:** Make 2 copies of everything before you go

#### Step 3.2: Choose a Bank

- **Action:** Select where you'll bank
- **Options:**
  - **(a) Your existing personal bank** — Easiest if you already have a relationship
  - **(b) Discover Business Banking** — https://www.discover.com/business-banking/ — No fees for 12 months
  - **(c) Blue Ridge Bank** — https://www.blueridgebank.com/ — Community-focused, serves Colorado

#### Step 3.3: Open the Account

- **Action:** Visit the bank (in-person or online)
- **Where to go:** Your chosen bank branch or website
- **What to do:**
  1. Tell them you want to open a business checking account for an LLC
  2. Provide your documents (EIN letter, Articles of Organization, ID)
  3. Complete the application
  4. Initial deposit (minimum $0-$100)
- **What to expect:** Debit card mailed within 5-7 business days; online access set up same day

#### Step 3.4: Activate Online Banking

- **Action:** Set up online access
- **Where to go:** Your bank's website or app
- **What to do:**
  1. Register for online banking
  2. Enable account alerts (recommended: daily balance notifications)
  3. Download the mobile app if available
- **What to expect:** Full access to view transactions, transfer funds, and download statements

## PHASE 4: BUSINESS ESSENTIALS
### Week 2

**Goal:** Set up the basic tools you need to communicate with clients and get paid.

#### Step 4.1: Get a Business Phone Number

- **Action:** Get a separate number for your business
- **Options:**
  - **(a) Google Voice** (FREE) — https://voice.google.com — Best for starting out
  - **(b) RingCentral** (Paid, ~$30/mo) — More features, professional

- **What to do (for Google Voice):**
  1. Go to https://voice.google.com
  2. Sign in with your existing Gmail account
  3. Click "Get a voice number"
  4. Search for an available number (consider a Colorado area code: 719 or 720)
  5. Verify your primary phone number
  6. Complete setup
- **What to expect:** A new phone number that forwards to your phone

#### Step 4.2: Set Up Business Email

- **Action:** Create a professional email address
- **Options:**
  - **(a) Free Gmail** — Create a new Gmail account: valleybuildabiz@gmail.com
  - **(b) Google Workspace** (~$12/mo) — More professional, includes Google Drive

- **What to do (for free Gmail):**
  1. Go to https://mail.google.com
  2. Create a new account with your business name
  3. Set up a signature: https://support.google.com/mail/answer/56256

- **What to expect:** A professional email address for client communication

#### Step 4.3: Create Email Signature

- **Action:** Create a consistent signature for all emails
- **What to include:**
  - Desirae [Your Name]
  - Owner, Valley Build-A-Biz LLC
  - Phone: (719) XXX-XXXX
  - Email: valleybuildabiz@gmail.com
  - Website: [your future website]
  - Tagline: "Growing small businesses in the San Luis Valley"

#### Step 4.4: Set Up Calendly for Client Bookings

- **Action:** Set up online scheduling
- **Where to go:** https://calendly.com
- **What to do:**
  1. Sign up for free account
  2. Connect your Google Voice number
  3. Create an event type: "Free Consultation" (15 or 30 minutes)
  4. Customize questions to ask during booking:
     - Business name
     - What service are you interested in?
     - Current challenges
  5. Copy your scheduling link for outreach materials
- **What to expect:** A link you can send to leads for booking: https://calendly.com/valleybuildabiz

## TROUBLESHOOTING: COMMON ISSUES

**Name already taken:** Use backup name "Build-A-Biz LLC" or try adding a modifier like "Valley Build-A-Biz Consulting LLC"

**Bank won't open account:** Make sure you have your EIN letter and Articles of Organization. Some banks require both.

**Can't verify Google Voice:** Make sure you're using a new Gmail account, not your personal one.

**Grants reject you:** First few rejections are normal. Refine your story and try again next month.

**No responses to outreach:** Vary your message, try calling instead of texting, ask for referrals.
