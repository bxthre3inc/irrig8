## 1. Colorado Water Court Context (Division 3)

### Court Jurisdiction
- **Water Court 3:** Alamosa District
- **Counties Served:** Rio Grande Basin, San Luis Valley
- **Specialization:** Compact compliance (Rio Grande Compact 1938)
- **Judge:** Water Division 3 presiding judge

### Rio Grande Compact Requirements
The 1938 Rio Grande Compact governs water apportionment between Colorado, New Mexico, and Texas. Key obligations:

| State | Obligation | Measurement Point |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| Colorado | Deliver sufficient water to NM | State line gages |
| New Mexico | Deliver to Elephant Butte Reservoir | Caballo Dam |
| Texas | Receive equitable share | Elephant Butte/El Paso |

### Current Compliance Crisis (2025-2026)
- **Texas v. New Mexico** (Supreme Court case, filed 2013): Alleged compact violations
- **Settlement reached** (August 2025): Groundwater Settlement Agreement signed
- **New Mexico water debt:** Mounting obligations to Texas under compact
- **Mexico treaty compliance:** 1944 treaty obligations under scrutiny

**Implication for Irrig8:** All SLV groundwater use is under heightened regulatory scrutiny. Telemetry evidence must demonstrate precise consumptive use calculations to support compact compliance.
