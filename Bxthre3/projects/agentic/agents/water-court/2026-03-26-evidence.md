## Context

### Rio Grande Compact Compliance
The San Luis Valley groundwater management is subject to Rio Grande Compact obligations. Texas v. New Mexico (settled August 2025) created precedent for interstate water enforcement. Irrig8 telemetry will be used to demonstrate:
- Groundwater depletion trends in SLV
- Accuracy of Irrig8 sensor network vs. manual measurement
- Compliance with decreed water rights

### Irrig8 System Context
Irrig8 is Bxthre3 Inc's end-to-end deterministic farming operating system, utilizing satellite data, in-ground sensor suites, soil variability maps, and neighboring system data. The SLV deployment is a flagship installation under scrutiny.
