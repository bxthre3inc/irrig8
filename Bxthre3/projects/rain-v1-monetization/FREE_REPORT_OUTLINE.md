### 4.1 High-Opportunity Areas
**Criteria:** Above-average precipitation + favorable timing

1. **Zone A: Northern Plains Extension**
   - Why: Extended spring moisture window
   - Crops benefited: Wheat, canola, pulse crops
   - Risk factors: Late frosts
   - Recommended actions

2. **Zone B: Midwest Corridor**
   - Why: Consistent summer rainfall predicted
   - Crops benefited: Corn, soybeans
   - Risk factors: Excess moisture, disease pressure
   - Recommended actions

3. **Zone C: Pacific Northwest**
   - Why: Moderated drought conditions
   - Crops benefited: Tree fruits, vineyards, wheat
   - Risk factors: Heat spikes
   - Recommended actions

4. **Zone D: Southeast Recovery**
   - Why: Return to normal patterns after drought
   - Crops benefited: Cotton, peanuts, vegetables
   - Risk factors: Hurricane season
   - Recommended actions

5. **Zone E: Texas Hill Country**
   - Why: Improved groundwater recharge predicted
   - Crops benefited: Livestock forage, pecans
   - Risk factors: Flash flooding
   - Recommended actions
