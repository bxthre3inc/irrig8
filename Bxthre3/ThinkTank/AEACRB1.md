## Product Definition

### Description
Combined offering: AgEaaS equipment subscription + Carbon8 soil carbon verification. Farmers get equipment access AND carbon credit revenue — full-farm operations as a service (OpEx model). One monthly fee covers equipment + water optimization + carbon verification.

### Problem Statement
Small-to-mid farms face three barriers simultaneously: (1) capital constraints for equipment, (2) no path to carbon credit revenue, (3) water optimization complexity. Existing solutions address each in isolation — no bundled full-farm OpEx offering exists.

### Solution Hypothesis
Bundled subscription model combining equipment access (AgEaaS) + soil carbon monitoring (Carbon8) + water optimization (Irrig8) into single monthly OpEx fee. Farmers avoid CapEx, earn carbon credits, reduce water costs — three revenue streams for Bxthre3 from one customer relationship.

### Target Users
- Small-to-mid farms in San Luis Valley seeking full-service farm operations
- Farmers transitioning from CapEx to OpEx mental model
- Regenerative agriculture practitioners seeking carbon credit revenue
- Irrig8 customers ready for equipment upgrade

### Success Metrics
- [ ] Customer LTV:target_customer_acquisition_cost > 5:1
- [ ] Bundled adoption rate: 40% of AgEaaS customers add Carbon8
- [ ] Carbon credit generation: 50 credits/acre/year verified
- [ ] Water reduction: 20% (existing Irrig8 metric)

### Estimated Scope
- **MVP:** 3-5 pilot farms (Q2 2026)
- **Regional:** 50 farms San Luis Valley (Q4 2026)
- **Expansion:** Colorado River Basin + Pacific Northwest (2027)
