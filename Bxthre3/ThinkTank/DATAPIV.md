## Product Definition

### Description
Aggregate anonymized field data from Irrig8 sensors (with farmer consent) and resell aggregated insights to agricultural inputs suppliers (seed, fertilizer, chemical companies). Creates recurring revenue from existing sensor infrastructure without farmer-facing product changes.

### Problem Statement
Ag-input companies (Corteva, Bayer, Nutrien) need ground-truth field data to improve targeting, but individual farm data is too small to matter. No intermediary aggregates and anonymizes SLV field data for commercial buyers.

### Solution Hypothesis
Become the data intermediary: (1) Partner with 10-20 Irrig8 farms to anonymize/aggregate field data, (2) Package into insights (soil moisture trends, yield correlations, input effectiveness), (3) Sell to ag-input companies for $2K-5K/quarter per insight type.

### Target Users
- **Buyers:** Ag-input companies wanting SLV ground-truth data
- **Partners:** Irrig8 farms (passive revenue share)
