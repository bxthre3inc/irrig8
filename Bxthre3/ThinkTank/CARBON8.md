## Product Definition

### Description
Extension of Irrig8 sensor infrastructure to enable soil carbon monitoring and verification for carbon credit markets. Dual revenue model: farmers save water via Irrig8 + earn carbon credit income through verified soil carbon sequestration.

### Problem Statement
[TBD]
Carbon farming market growing 14.5% CAGR ($642M → $2.17B by 2034), but no automated soil carbon verification at scale exists. Farmers cannot access carbon credit revenue without expensive manual soil testing.

### Solution Hypothesis
[TBD]
Extend existing Irrig8 in-ground sensor suites with soil carbon sensors to provide continuous, automated carbon verification. Integration with carbon credit registries enables farmers to monetize regenerative practices.

- Farmers seeking carbon credit revenue streams
