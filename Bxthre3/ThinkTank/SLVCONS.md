## Product Definition

### Description
Low-capital advisory service: provide water optimization consulting to SLV farms using publicly available satellite data (Landsat/Sentinel — free) + basic analysis. No hardware, no sensors, just expertise + data. Revenue from consulting fees immediately.

### Problem Statement
SLV farmers face increasing water scarcity but lack technical expertise to interpret satellite data for irrigation optimization. Large consultants charge too much; DIY is too complex. Gap for affordable, data-informed water advisory.

### Solution Hypothesis
Offer tiered consulting: (1) Basic audit — free (demonstrates value), (2) Full analysis — $500-1,500/farm, (3) Ongoing monitoring — $200/month. Uses free satellite APIs + Irrig8 methodology without requiring Irrig8 hardware purchase.

### Target Users
- Small-to-mid farms in SLV (< 500 acres)
- Farms unable to afford full Irrig8 hardware deployment
- Farms exploring water optimization before committing to sensor investment
