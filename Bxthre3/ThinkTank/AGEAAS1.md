## Product Definition

### Description
Subscription-based agriculture equipment access model for San Luis Valley farmers. Eliminates upfront capital barriers by providing equipment (tractors, implements, precision ag tools) via monthly/seasonal subscription — extending the "water-as-a-service" concept to "equipment-as-a-service."

### Problem Statement
[TBD]
Agriculture equipment market $312.7B by 2033, but upfront capital barriers constrain adoption for small-to-mid farms. No dominant player offering true subscription/HaaS in agriculture.

### Solution Hypothesis
[TBD]
OpEx model for agriculture equipment — farmers pay monthly/seasonal fees for equipment access, maintenance, and upgrades. Bundled with Irrig8 for "full farm operations as a service."
