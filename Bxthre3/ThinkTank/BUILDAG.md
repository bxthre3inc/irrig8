## Product Definition

### Description
Extension of Valley Build-A-Biz LLC's venture studio model specifically for agriculture entrepreneurs. Combines business formation, mentorship, and now equipment access (AgEaaS) — creating full-stack farm venture creation pipeline. New farmers get: entity formation + business plan + equipment subscription + Irrig8 water optimization.

### Problem Statement
Aspiring farmers face fragmented support: entity lawyers, business mentors, equipment financing, water rights, and ag tech — no single entity provides coordinated farm venture creation. Existing programs address one component at a time.

### Solution Hypothesis
Vertical integration of farm venture creation: Build-A-Biz provides business formation + mentorship + AgEaaS equipment access + Irrig8 technology. New farms launch "operations-ready" with equipment, water optimization, and ongoing support — not fragmented across 5+ vendors.

### Target Users
- First-generation farmers in Colorado SLV
- Farm transition families (succession planning)
- Agricultural entrepreneurs seeking turnkey farm operations
- Veterans/retirees starting farm ventures

### Success Metrics
- [ ] Ventures launched per year: 10 (Year 1), 25 (Year 2), 50 (Year 3)
- [ ] Retention rate: 80% after 3 years
- [ ] Equipment subscription attach rate: 70%

### Estimated Scope
- **Phase 1:** 5 pilot ventures (Q2-Q3 2026)
- **Phase 2:** 25 ventures/year (2027)
- **Phase 3:** National expansion (2028)
