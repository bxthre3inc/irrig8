# Bxthre3 Workforce Foundation
> 501(c)(3) Public Benefit Corporation

## Mission
Ensure automation creates more prosperity than it destroys.

## Funding
- 2% of gross automation savings (Bxthre3 Inc contribution)
- Industry partner matching
- Grant funding

## Programs
1. **ReSkill Academy** - Free training for displaced workers
2. **Job Bridge** - Interview guarantees with Bxthre3 vendors
3. **Income Support** - Gap coverage during retraining

## Governance
Independent board: 50% workers, 50% industry. Annual public report.
