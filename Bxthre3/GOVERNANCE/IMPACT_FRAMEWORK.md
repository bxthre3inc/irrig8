# Bxthre3 Impact Measurement Framework

## Net Job Calculus
| Metric | Formula | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Gross Jobs Created | Direct + Indirect | +30 engineers |
| Gross Jobs Displaced | Roles automated | -2000 packers |
| Net Employment | Created - Displaced | -1970 |
| Wage Multiplier | New wage / Old wage | 2.66x |

## Timeline Language
- Pre-Impact: "Will create..."
- Post-Impact: "Created..."
- Net Positive: "Resulted in..."

## Workforce Foundation
- 501c3 funded by 2% automation savings
- Upskills displaced workers
- Guaranteed interviews
- Income bridge coverage
