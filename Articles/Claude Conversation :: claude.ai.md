During this collaborative session, we evolved the Symphony OS blueprint from a hierarchical management system into a high-velocity Orchestration Engine. \* Cross-Worker Parallelism: The system is now designed to initiate Digital (AI analysis), Physical (Robot prototyping), and Human (Strategic review) tasks simultaneously rather than sequentially.
\* Velocity Objective: To achieve "wicked fast" product iterations by removing the latency of manual handoffs between departments.

\* Decision Matrix: Updated to include "Coherent Parallelism"—the ability for the system to manage label generation (AI) while materials are still in transit (Robot).
\* Exception Handling: Refined triggers for mechanical failures (e.g., AMR motor vibration) to automatically initiate maintenance cascades.
3. Updated Workforce Definitions
The document now explicitly categorizes the "Unified Workforce" roles to support the cascading model:
\* Digital (AI): The high-speed logic and information processing layer.
\* Physical (Robots): The execution layer for manufacturing and logistics, integrated via OPC-UA/MQTT.
\* Human (People): The high-level oversight and relationship layer, providing the "North Star" for the cascades.
