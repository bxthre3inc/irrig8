# Irrig8 — Business Plan

## Overview
Irrig8 — The Deterministic Farming Operating System
**Sector:** Global Water Infrastructure

## Problem
Agricultural water management is broken. Farmers lack legally defensible, real-time irrigation data. Colorado River Compact cuts are coming. Water rights trading requires court-admissible records.

## Solution
End-to-end IoT sensor platform for center-pivot irrigation:
- Satellite data + ground sensor suites + neighboring system data
- Real-time water rights compliance monitoring
- PBFT blockchain consensus for water court admissibility
- Water rights trading marketplace

## Stack
LoRa mesh, edge compute (Raspberry Pi CM4 + ESP32-S3), FHE encryption, LRZ1/LRZ2 sensor devices

## Traction
- Pilot targeting Q1-Q2 2026 (CSU 2-field validation)
- Patent portfolio: Seasonal Sled, Fisherman's Attention Engine, Digital Water Ledger, Band-It Rapid Mounting
- Grant: ESTCP $1.5M application in progress

## Revenue Model
Hardware sales + SaaS subscription + water rights trading marketplace fee

## Flywheel With GovCon Intel
GovCon Intel finds USDA EQIP grants + DOE water technology funding for Irrig8.
